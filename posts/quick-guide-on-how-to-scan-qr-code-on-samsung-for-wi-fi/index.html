Making it easy to enjoy the convenience of Wi-Fi on your Samsung phone is as simple as scanning a QR code. Start off by enabling Wi-Fi settings on your device. You can also change settings like network name, frequency and security settings.

The next step is to scan the QR code for the network. Open up the camera app on your device and point it directly at the QR code. Your phone should instantly recognize the code and a small pop-up window should appear. From there, select Connect to Wi-Fi and you should be connected in no time.

You can also find the network list on your device and manually connect. Open the Settings panel of your device and select the Wi-Fi option. Here you will find all the available networks in your area. Finally, you can also manually add the network by tapping on the Add network option. Enter the SSID name for the network, the security settings and the password, then press Connect. This is the last step in connecting to a Wi-Fi network on your Samsung device.
