1. Understand How to Capture a Screenshot#

Taking a screenshot of your WhatsApp status is a useful way to save and share the content with your contacts. With just a few simple steps, you will be surprised how easy it is to capture a screenshot on your smartphone. Here are the steps to do it:

Firstly, open WhatsApp and go to the status section. Find the post you want to screenshot and press and hold the power button and the volume down button at the same time. On some Android devices, you may have to press the home button and the power button at the same time.

Secondly, you will see a brief animation. The screenshot has been captured and saved as an image in your gallery. When you open your gallery, you will find the screenshot in the WhatsApp folder.

Thirdly, you can also send your screenshot directly to your preferred contact. When you have taken a screenshot, you can find a sharing icon at the bottom of the post. This will help you to forward the screenshot to any of your WhatsApp contacts.

Lastly, you will also be able to open the screenshot with any other app. You will find the sharing icon at the top right corner of the post. This will help you to choose the app you want to open it with.
