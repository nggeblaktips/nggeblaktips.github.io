4. Taking Screenshots using Assistive Touch#

Taking screenshots on your iPhone 12 Pro doesn’t have to be complicated. Here are five simple steps to capture your screen using Assistive Touch:

  1. Open the Settings app. Navigate to General, then Accessibility, and then select Assistive Touch.

  2. Toggle Assistive Touch On. An icon of a circle with a white border will appear on the Home and Lock screens.

  3. Tap the Assistive Touch icon. A menu will appear with a list of options. Select the ‘Device’ option.

  4. Select the ‘More’ option. This will open a list of options. Choose the ‘Screenshot’ option from the list to take a screenshot of your screen.
