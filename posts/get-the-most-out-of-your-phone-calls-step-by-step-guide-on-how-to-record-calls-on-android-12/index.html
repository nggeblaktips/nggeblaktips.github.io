Next, open the Call log tab and select the call you want to record. Once you have selected the call, you will find an option to record the call. Tap the Record button and the call will start recording. You will see the recording sign on the top corner of the screen.

Third, when the call is finished, you will get a notification saying the recording has been completed. You can now access the recorded call from call history under the recorded calls tab. Tap the call to play it and it will be saved to your device.

Finally, if you want to transfer the recorded call to your computer, you can do so by transferring the recorded call using Bluetooth. Go to Settings and then select the Bluetooth setting. Connect your computer to your Android device and transfer the recorded call to your computer.
