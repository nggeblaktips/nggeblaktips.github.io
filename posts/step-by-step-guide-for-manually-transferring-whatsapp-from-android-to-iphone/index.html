After the backup is complete, open the Android device, open Google Drive, and select the “WhatsApp” folder. Select the “WhatsApp Backups” folder, and then copy the “msgstore.db.crypt” file to the iPhone.

Finally, connect the iPhone to the computer, open iTunes, and select the device. Go to “Manually manage videos, photos, and music” and copy the “msgstore.db.crypt” file from the iPhone to the computer. Open WhatsApp on the iPhone and restore the chat from the copied file.
