1. What is DPI?#

DPI stands for dots per inch, and is an important concept for image quality. High DPI images look sharper and clearer, while low DPI images may appear pixelated and blurry. Checking the DPI of an image on an Android device is very easy. It can be done in six simple steps.

First, open the image you want to check in the Gallery application on your Android device. Secondly, tap on the menu button at the top right corner of the screen. Thirdly, select “Details” from the menu. Fourthly, a dialog box will appear that shows the DPI of the image. Fifthly, the width and height of the image are also displayed in the dialog box. Sixthly, close the dialog to exit.

Using these 6 steps, you can easily check the DPI of any image on your Android device. It is important to note that the DPI of an image may be different from the actual physical size of the image. The physical size can be adjusted in the image editor.
