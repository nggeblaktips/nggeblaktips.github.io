Q1. What is the easiest way to capture iPad Air 4 screen?

A1. The easiest way to capture iPad Air 4 screen is by using the built-in shortcut combination on the device. To capture your screen press “Home + Power/Sleep” button at the same time. The screen will flash and a preview of your screenshot will appear in the bottom-left corner of the display.

Q2. How can I share my iPad Air 4 screenshot?

A2. After taking the screenshot, simply tap the preview in the bottom-left corner and select the “Share” button. From there, you can select which method of sharing you’d like to use like Facebook, Twitter, iMessage, Mail, or AirDrop.

Q3. How can I take a portion of the screen?

A3. To take a portion of the screen, press the “Home + Power/Sleep” buttons at the same time, then use your finger to select the area of the display you’d like to capture. Once you’ve chosen the area of the screen, it will be saved to the Camera Roll.

Q4. How do I find my screenshots?

A4. Your iPad Air 4 saves all screenshots to the Camera Roll. You can find the Camera Roll by opening the Photos app and selecting the “Albums” tab. Then select “Camera Roll” to view all of your iPad Air 4 screenshots.

Q5. How do I take a screenshot on an external display?

A5. To take a screenshot on an external display, connect the display to your iPad Air 4 and use the same “Home + Power/Sleep” button shortcut to take the screenshot. The screenshot will be saved to the Camera Roll.
