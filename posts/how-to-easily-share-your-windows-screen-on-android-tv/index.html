The third step is to open the ‘Display Settings’ tab found on your Android TV. Choose the menu labelled ‘Screen Mirroring’ and establish the desired connection.

The fourth and final step is to open the ‘Device’ tab from the Windows settings app. Click the ‘Connected Devices’ option and then select your Android TV from the list of available devices. This will allow you to share your Windows desktop to your Android TV!
