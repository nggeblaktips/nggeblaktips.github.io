I. How to Clear Cache on Fire TV Box#

Clearing your Fire TV Box cache is a quick and easy way to improve the performance of your Fire TV device. The cache of a Fire TV device stores information from recently used apps, which can slow down the device if it becomes too full. In this guide, we’ll take you through the steps of clearing your Fire TV box cache for faster streaming.

Firstly, you will need to open the Fire TV Menu. From here, you can select the ‘Settings’ option and then ‘Applications’ from the menu. Once you have opened the Applications menu, you will need to select the ‘Manage Installed Applications’ option.

When you have selected ‘Manage Installed Applications’, you will need to scroll through the list of apps and select the one you wish to clear the cache from. Once you have clicked on the app, you will be presented with a new menu. Here, you will need to select the ‘Clear Cache’ option which is usually located near the bottom.

Finally, after selecting ‘Clear Cache’, you will need to confirm the selection by selecting ‘OK’. Your Fire TV box’s cache for the selected app has now been cleared. If you wish to clear the cache of more apps, simply repeat the same steps with the other apps.

Clearing your Fire TV box cache is a great way to improve the performance and speed of your device. The cache should be cleared at least once a month to keep the device running at peak performance. With this short guide, you should now be able to clear cache from the Fire TV box with ease.
