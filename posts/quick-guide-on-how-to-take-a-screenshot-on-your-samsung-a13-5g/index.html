1. Explanation on What a Screenshot Is#

A screenshot is the action of taking a photo of what is currently displayed on your device’s screen. When you take a screenshot, the image of the screen is saved and stored in the device’s memory or External device. This is useful if you want to keep a record of something that appears on your screen. On Samsung A13 5G, it is easy to take a screenshot with just a few taps. Here is a quick guide on how to take a screenshot on the Samsung A13 5G.

First, press and hold down the Power button and Volume Down button at the same time for a few seconds. You should hear a click or a shutter sound. That means the screenshot has been taken successfully.

Second, the screenshot will be automatically saved in your Gallery, in the Screenshot folder. To view it, open your Gallery app, and find the Screenshot folder. Tap it to view all of the screenshots that you’ve taken.

Third, you can also access the screenshots more conveniently by swiping down from the top of your phone’s screen to open the notification panel. All of your screenshots will appear here, so you can tap them to view, open, or share.

Finally, if you want to edit the screenshot you have taken, press and hold the screenshot in the notification panel. A few options will appear at the bottom of the screen. Choose an option to edit the screenshot, and make changes to the screenshot before you share it.
