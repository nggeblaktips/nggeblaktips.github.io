To make the most of the Hisense Android TV experience, it is essential to scan for available channels. To help you, here is a step-by-step guide on how to scan channels on Hisense Android TV.

Firstly, press the ‘Settings’ button on the remote control. In the ‘Settings’ menu, scroll down to the ‘TV Settings’ option, and select it. Then, select the ‘Deleting Channels’ option. After that, select the ‘Scan Channels’ option.

Secondly, a new window will appear. In this window, you can select how you want to scan: Air or Cable. After selecting your choice, you can also adjust the signal strength by using the arrow buttons on the screen. Once everything is adjusted, press the ‘OK’ button.

Thirdly, Hisense Android TV will start to search for available channels. Once finished, you will be able to view all available channels by scrolling with the arrow keys. To make sure that it worked, try to select any channel you just scanned. If it works, it means that everything went as expected.

Lastly, after you finish, you can save the scanned channels by pressing the ‘OK’ button. Then, press the ‘Return’ button twice. Congratulations! You have just learned how to scan for TV channels on your Hisense Android TV.
