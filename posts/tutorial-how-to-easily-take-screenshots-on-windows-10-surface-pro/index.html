1. Introduction to Windows 10 Surface Pro#

The Microsoft Surface Pro is one of the most popular 2-in-1 laptop hybrid machines on the market, offering features like long battery life, great performance, and versatility. With Windows 10 on the Surface Pro, it’s easy to take a screenshot, whether you want to capture a web page, a game, or something else. Here’s a brief guide on how to take screenshots on your Windows 10 Surface Pro.

The first step is to launch the taskbar and select the Snipping Tool. This will allow you to take a screenshot of the whole screen or any active window. To take a screenshot of the whole screen, press the Windows key and Print Screen (PrtScr) together. The screenshot will be saved in your Pictures library.

If you want to capture a specific area of the screen, use the Snipping Tool and select New. Then you can drag your mouse to select the area you’d like to capture. Once done, you can save the screenshot in the desired format, such as JPEG, PNG, or GIF.

You can also use the Surface Pen to take a screenshot. Simply open the Charms Bar by pressing the Windows + C keys and select the Camera icon. The screenshot will be saved in your Pictures library.

Finally, if you want to take a screenshot of an active window, hold down Ctrl + Alt and press the PrtScn. The screenshot will be saved in your clipboard where you can paste it into any image editor.

These are some of the simple and easy ways to take screenshots on your Windows 10 Surface Pro. With these tips, you can easily capture whatever you need to capture on your device.
