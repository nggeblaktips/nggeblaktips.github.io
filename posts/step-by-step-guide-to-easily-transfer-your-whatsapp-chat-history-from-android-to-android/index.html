2. Steps to Transfer WhatsApp Chat History from Android to Android#

Transfer WhatsApp chat history from one Android device to another is now easier than ever. Follow these simple steps to move your WhatsApp history with ease:

  1. On your old device, open WhatsApp and go to settings in the chat screen.

  2. Tap on “Chats” and then tap on “Chat history” followed by “Export”.

  3. Select “Email chat” to email the entire WhatsApp chat history from your old device to your new device.

  4. On your new device, open the email that was sent to you with the chat history from the old device and download the attachment. Then, open the attachment and follow the instructions to import the chat history to your new device.

With these simple steps, you can easily transfer your WhatsApp chat history from one Android device to another.
