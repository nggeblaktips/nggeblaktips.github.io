1.How to Connect iPhone and Android Tablet#

Here’s a step-by-step guide to getting your apps from your iPhone onto your Android tablet. Firstly, enable the Trusted Devices feature on your iPhone by going into Settings and tapping on your Apple ID. After that, register your Android tablet on the list of trusted devices on your iPhone. Next, install the Google Drive app on both your iPhone and Android tablet from the App Store and the Google Play Store, respectively. Finally, open the Google Drive app on your iPhone, and move your desired apps into the cloud storage. Once this is done, use the same Google Drive account to log in on your Android tablet, and you’re good to go! With these simple steps, you can easily transfer and connect your iPhone and Android tablet.
