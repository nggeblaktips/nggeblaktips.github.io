2. Enable AssistiveTouch for Taking a Screenshot Without a Power Button#

Taking a screenshot on an iPhone 8 without a power button is possible with the use of AssistiveTouch. AssistiveTouch, among other features, allows iPhone 8 users to take screenshots. Here’s how:

Open the Settings app and navigate to General > Accessibility > AssistiveTouch. Toggle AssistiveTouch to ‘On’. A white dot will appear onscreen. Tap the dot to open the AssistiveTouch menu.

Tap ‘Device’ on the AssistiveTouch menu, and then tap the ‘More’ option. Tap the ‘Screenshot’ icon and your iPhone 8 will take a screenshot. The screenshot will appear in the Photos app.

To disable AssistiveTouch, open the Settings app and navigate to General > Accessibility > AssistiveTouch. Toggle AssistiveTouch to ‘Off’. You can also tap and hold the AssistiveTouch icon and select ‘Remove’.

Enabling AssistiveTouch as described above makes it easy to take a screenshot on an iPhone 8 without a power button. With the feature enabled, you can capture your screen anytime without having to press any buttons.

Q2 : Is the process different if I don’t have a power button? A2 : No, the process for taking a screenshot remains the same even if the power button is not working.

Q4 : Can I edit my screenshots? A4 : Yes, iPhone 8 comes with a built-in screenshot editor. To access it, open the screenshot after you capture it and click on the Edit button at the top right corner of the screen. From there, you can crop, rotate, or make other changes to your screenshot.

Q5 : Are my screenshots stored on iCloud? A5 : By default, screenshots are stored on the iPhone itself and not on iCloud. If you want to save your screenshots on iCloud, go to the Settings and click on Your Profile and then tap on iCloud. Then scroll down and toggle on the iCloud Drive option under Photos. After that, you can share your screenshots on iCloud.
