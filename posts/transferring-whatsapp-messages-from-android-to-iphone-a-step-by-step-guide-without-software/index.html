1. Traditional way to transfer WhatsApp messages from Android to iPhone#

Transferring WhatsApp Messages from Android to iPhone can be done with relative ease without the need for any third-party software. Here is a step-by-step guide to help users complete the process. Next, transfer the WhatsApp backup file from the Android device to the iPhone. To do this, first connect the Android device to your computer and locate the WhatsApp backup file from the Internal Memory or SD Card. After you have found the file, copy it and transfer it to the iPhone, either using iTunes, iCloud, or email.
