a. Enabling Voice Memo#

Voice memos are a great way to keep a record of your thoughts or important points while on the go. To save or share your newly recorded voice memo on android device, here’s what you need to do.

First, open the Voice Recorder app in your Android device. Tap on the ‘Options’ icon in the top right corner of the app. Select the ‘My recordings’ option to view the recordings you have created.

In the recordings list, long-press on the clip you want to share. A pop-up menu will appear. Tap Share or Save to save your recording to an external drive.

To share your clip, select one of the sharing options from the same menu. You can share via email, Bluetooth, third-party apps, or social media. Once selected, follow the prompts to complete the sharing process.

Finally, press the ‘X’ button to close the menu and return to your list of recordings. You have now successfully shared or saved your voice memo on Android.
