2. Editing and Sharing Screenshots on iPad#

Taking screenshots on iPad devices has become easier than ever before. In this article, we will be providing you with a step-by-step guide on how to take screenshot on iPad. First, press and release the Sleep/Wake button on the top right side of the iPad. Secondly, press and release the Home button on the front of the iPad. Your screen should now flash white and record an image of your current screen. To view your screenshot, open the Photos application and select the Camera Roll album. Here, you will be able to view the screenshot you just took. Finally, if you’d like to share the screenshot with others, tap the Share button and select the option that you prefer. With this your screenshot will be sent to the other device. So, if you ever need to take a screenshot on your iPad device, just follow the steps above and you’re all set!
