Q.2 Are there any other methods to remove split-screen on my Android tablet?

Q.3 Is there any way to bring back the split-screen view on my Android tablet?

A. Yes, you can re-enable the split-screen view on your Android tablet by following the same steps mentioned above. Go to your device’s Settings app, then select the ‘Display’ option, and finally tap on the ‘Split-screen mode’ switch to enable it. After that, the split-screen view should reappear on your device.

Q.4 What should I do if I want to use more than two apps in split-screen mode on my Android tablet?

A. If you want to use more than two apps in split-screen mode on your Android tablet, you can try using the ‘Split Multi-Window’ feature. To do that, go to the ‘Settings’ app, select the ‘Display’ option, open the ‘Split Multi-Window’ option, and switch the toggle to enable it. After that, you should be able to use more than two apps in the split-screen view.
