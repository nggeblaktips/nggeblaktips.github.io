1. Access the Screenshot Tool#

Taking a screenshot on your iPad Air 5 is easy! Here are the 4 simple steps to access the screenshot tool:

First, locate the power button and the home button. The power button is located on the top right side of the tablet, while the home button is located right below the tablet’s screen.

Second, press and hold both the power and the home buttons at the same time for a couple of seconds until you hear a camera shutter sound. This indicates that the screenshot has been taken.

Third, open the iPad’s photos app to view all your screenshots. From the home page, go to ‘Albums’ and then select the ‘Screenshots’ album.

Fourth, you can either share your screenshot or edit it to your liking. Simply locate the image and select the options like ‘Share’ or ‘Edit’ at the bottom of the screen.

With these four simple steps, you can take a screenshot easily on your iPad Air 5 today.
