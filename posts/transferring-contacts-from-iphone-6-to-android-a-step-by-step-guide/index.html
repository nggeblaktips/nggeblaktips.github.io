Copying contacts from your iPhone 6 to your brand-new Android device is relatively easy. To begin, you’ll need to ensure both your iPhone 6 as well as your Android device are connected to the same Wi-Fi network. Once connected, open the Contacts app on your iPhone 6 and tap the three dots located in the top right corner of the screen. Tap the option that says “Share” followed by “Share Contacts” before finally choosing “Share vCard.” Connect to your Android device via Bluetooth and then accept the request to transfer data. After the transfer is completed, open the Contacts app on your Android device and check that all contacts have been successfully transferred. 2. Transferring Contacts with Google Contacts Sync#

If you’re switching from an iPhone 6 to an Android device, you can easily transfer your contacts with Google Contacts Sync. All you need to do is enable contact syncing on your iPhone 6 and Android device so that the contacts can get synced to your Google account. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do that:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone 6 and tap your name at the top.
  2. From the Apple ID page, tap iCloud and turn on the Contacts toggle.
  3. On your Android device, open the Settings app and go to Accounts & Sync.
  4. Tap Add Account -> Google and fill in your Gmail credentials.
  5. Sign in and make sure the Contacts toggle is turned on.
  6. You’re done! All your iPhone 6’s contacts are now synced to your Google account and are ready to be used on your Android device.

3. Transferring Contacts Manually#

Transferring contacts from an iPhone 6 to an Android device is a straightforward process that anyone can do. All that is needed is a USB cable and a computer with iTunes installed. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to do it:

  1. Connect your iPhone 6 to your computer using the USB cable.
  2. Open iTunes on your computer.
  3. Click on the device icon at the top of the window.
  4. Select ‘Info’ and then click on ‘Sync Contacts’. All your contacts from the iPhone 6 will now be transferred to your computer.
