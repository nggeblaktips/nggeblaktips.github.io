1. Overview on How to Screenshot on an iPhone 6 Plus Without the Power Button#

Taking screenshots on an iPhone 6 Plus without the power button is not as difficult as it may sound. By following these five easy steps, you can easily capture screenshots on your device.

First, open the app or website that you would like to screenshot. Press the Home and Volume Up buttons at the same time. You will know the screenshot has been taken when you hear a shutter sound and see a thumbnail in the lower corner of the screen.

Then, open the Photos app and locate the screenshot. Tap on the thumbnail to view it in full-screen mode. You can also find it in the Album tab of the Photos app.

Finally, share and edit the screenshot as you like. To share the image, either tap the Share button in the upper right corner or press and hold on the screenshot to bring up the Share Dialog. You can also crop and annotate the image by tapping the Edit button in the upper right corner.
