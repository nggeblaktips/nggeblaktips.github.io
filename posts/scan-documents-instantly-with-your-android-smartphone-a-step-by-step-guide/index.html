3. Tips for Achieving Quality Scans#

2. Scanning the Document#

Scanning documents has become easier than ever before. For those who have access to an Android phone, it is possible to scan a document without using an external scanner or a printer. To get a high-quality scan, this article shares some useful tips to follow.

Firstly, use a good quality camera for scanning a document. This helps to capture more details, which will allow the document to be clearer when printed or saved. Keep the camera steady while photographing the document as a certainty of a blur-free image.

Secondly, when capturing the document, make sure the background of the image is well lit and clear. This will help the text to standout and be more legible. While taking the picture, make sure to not have any other items or objects in the background, which can distract from the subject.

Thirdly, shoot the document at the highest resolution possible. A higher resolution scan enables the image to be printed better and retain detail. For a better zoom-in, shooting at a higher resolution will allow for a better stock of information.

Lastly, use an editing tool to further enhance the document image. Tools like Adobe Lightroom, Snapseed etc. help to adjust brightness, contrast, crop, blur, and sharpen the image. As the final step, save the image in the preferred format, such as JPG or PDF, so that the document can be sent or printed.
