Q: What is a screenshot? A: A screenshot is a digital image of your phone’s display. It is taken with the press of a button or combination of buttons, and allows you to capture the contents of a screen quickly and easily.

Q: How do I take a screenshot on my Motorola Android phone? A: You can take a screenshot by pressing the Power and Volume Down buttons simultaneously. Hold the buttons down for at least a second, and you will hear a shutter sound as the screenshot is taken.

Q: How do I access my screenshots? A: Once taken, the screenshot will be stored in your phone’s Gallery app in a folder named “Screenshots”.

Q: How do I share my screenshots? A: You can share your screenshots easily in a few ways. To share a screenshot right after you’ve taken it, just pull down the notification bar and tap the screenshot. This will open up a menu with several sharing options.

Q: Where are my screenshots saved? A: As mentioned above, your screenshots will be saved in the “Screenshots” folder in the Gallery app. You can also navigate to the DCIM folder and look for a folder named “Screenshots”.
