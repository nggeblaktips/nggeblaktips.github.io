Fortunately, you don’t need to download or install any extra app – screen recording is built-in and ready for you to use. Here are the steps to help you get started with screen recording on your Android phone.

First, access the quick settings menu. Swipe down the notification bar from the top of the screen. Tap the edit icon on the settings shortcut, and then tap the screen recording shortcut to move it to the quick settings menu.

Second, start recording. After adding the screen recording shortcut to the quick settings menu, just swipe down the notification bar again, and then tap the screen recording shortcut. It will start recording your screen. To stop, swipe down the notification bar again, and tap that shortcut again.

Third, configure settings. To configure settings for screen recordings, go to the settings and scroll down to select Advanced features then click Motions and gestures. Next, scroll down, and tap Smart capture, then Record screen. Here, you can configure the settings for screen recordings.

Fourth, access the recording. To access the recording, open your gallery app and select Albums. You will find a Screen recordings folder. Open it to find your recordings.
